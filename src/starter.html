Content Panes

Need a way to include more robust, content-based panes in your sidebar navigation? No problem. Bond includes a pre-built pane specifically made to accept any type of content you throw at it. This is especially useful for creating a Contact pane with multiple addresses, a form, and even a map!

Content panes adapt automatically to the length of your content so don't worry about fitting it all into one pane. If your content exceeds the device's height, it will automatically become scrollable.

Broken Images

If the path to any image is broken or missing, Bond gracefully handles the fallback by informing your user that the image was not found, along with the title and URL.



Responsive Embeds

All forms of embeds are supported and are responsive out of the box. Whether you're using Vimeo, YouTube, Daily Motion or any other type of embed, the frame will automatically scale and maintain aspect ratio on any device.




Responsive Tables

All tables inserted into a WYSIWYG will automatically become responsive with Bond. At a certain device width the table becomes horizontally scrollable within a parent container to ensure mobile users are able to comfortably view it.

Dialog Box

Bond's dialog boxes are set to a maximum height which overflows gracefully when exceeded. This means that the contents of the dialog box can be anything from forms, paragraphs, images, video and more.

In addition, the dialog can easily be closed in a variety of ways including clicking the close button in the top-right corner, clicking the overlay outside of the dialog, or by pressing the Esc key.
